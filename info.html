NATSUKI ONEYAMA MAKE-UP ARTIST

BIOGRAPHY


Natsuki Oneyama is a Paris-based makeup artist born in Japan, working across editorial, advertising and selected commercial projects.

Influenced by both Japanese and French visual cultures, his approach to beauty is rooted in clean, modern aesthetics, with a strong sensitivity to skin, texture and natural light.

His work focuses on refined, contemporary beauty with an understated approach to image-making.
